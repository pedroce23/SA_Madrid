Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to go abroad?

First, you must be an undergraduate student with a major or minor in the Olin Business School. Most studets go abroad during their junior year, but sophomores and seniors are welcome. We prefer students to have completed at least SPAN 307D. 2 letters of recommendation, from professors or advisors. It is also advisable that you have most of your core business requirements completed by the time you depart for Madrid.

When is the deadline to apply for Spring 2016?

The application deadline is May 1, 2015. The deadlie for the Fall 2016 semester is due February 2, 2016.

How many classes will I take abroad?

Most students take 4-6 classes while abroad, in some combination of CES and Grados classes. All students will be required to take a Spanish language class. Many Grados classes can fulfill your Olin major and minor requiremets (With advisor approval required).

Where in Madrid will I live?

All students will have the opportunity to choose between living in a homestay with a family, or in an apartment with other students. A benefit of the Olin Madrid Study Abroad is that all the housing options are very centrally located. We have a pool of families and apartments that have hosted students in the past all located in great locations. Students should be cofident they will liver in/near Puerta del Sol, Plaza Mayor, Retiro, La Latina, Moncloa, or Salamanca.

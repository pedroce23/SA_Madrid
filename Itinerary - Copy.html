Program Itinerary Most Important Dates

Arrival and First Month in Sevilla

You'll arrive in Madrid on the first Saturday or Sunday in January 2016. After a night in a hostel in the center of Madrid, you'll meet with your ACCENT program directors. On Monday you'll take a high-speed train south to Sevilla, where your whole group will stay in a Jersey Shore style house complex. You'll be in Sevilla for two weeks brushing up on your Spanish in ENFOREX business Spanish language class. You'll then return to Madrid, move into your homestays and register for classes. You'll begin attending UC3M in late January.

Program Trips and Cultural Events

Included in the study abroad experience are the following trips: 3 day trip to Brussels to meet with EU officials and presents findings for European Study Tour (EST) project, weekend trip to Barcelona in March (includes city tour), one day trip by bus to Toledo to visit the ancient city (city tour included), optional bull fighting event in Madrid, Spanish cooking classes hosted at the ACCENT center, and much more!

Class Schedule and Spring Break

You will register for classes at UC3M in the second to last week of January, and then begin class during the last week of January. Spring break will take place during the first week of April. Finals for CES class will take place at the end of April. Grados classes will continue through the first two weeks of May, with finals taking place between May 23 and June 2.

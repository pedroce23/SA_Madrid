Universidad Carlos III de Madrid

About UC3M

  • Largest University in Madrid
  • Main Campus located in Getafe
  • Grado and CES classes offered
  • Mandatory Spanish language class
  • Visit the official website

    • Classes

    Grado classes will immerse you with local Spaniards. These course, taught in English or Spanish, will fulfill your undergrad business requirements. CES courses are all taught in Spanish, but are catered towards study abroad students. These class mainly focus on Spanish art, history, geography, and politics.

    Facilities

    As a study abroad student, you will receive one free credit for an extracurricular activity of your choosing. Activities include: access to a state-of-the-art gym with weights and cardio equipment, membership in a dance class, membership of a club sports team. All study abroad students can utilize the library and 3 dining halls.

    Location

    All courses will be located on the main campus located in Getafe, a suburb of Madrid. All students will live in Madrid proper with closeby access to the metro. Students will metro to the Sol or Atocha stations, where they will transfer to the apropriate light-rail train towards Getafe. After arriving, there is a 10min walk.

Testimonials

"Loved experiencing real Spanish culture..."

"The cooking classes were so fun!! I loved learning how to make paella and churros and then cooking them for my host mom's birthday. She was SOO surprised!! Also salsa Mondays were so fun it was great being able to impress the locals with my dance moves!" - Maria, Spring 2014

"My Spanish got so much better!"

"When I first got to Madrid I was afraid to say anything for fear of embarrassing myself. Making Spanish friends on campus really helped and I also learned so much taking my marketing class in Spanish. Although it was intimidating at first to be the only non-native speaker in a class of 40, my professor and groupmates really helped me out. I'm so glad I did it." - Rosco, Fall 2013

"I made so many new friends!"

"When I went abroad I only kind of new a couple kids on my program. Throughout the course of the semester, our whole group became really close and we had a lot of fun together- in class and traveling. I also made friends with other Americans studying abroad in Madrid through my CES classes. I used the basketball court to meet local Spaniards." - Miyoko, Spring 2014.
